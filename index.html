Past Projects

Teaching Quests in Costa Rica

Education is the key to unlocking the vast potential that all humans share. Access to even basic education resources is crucial and can be life-altering. Volunteers can teach English, math, science, environmental studies, and even coach sports.

Community Development Quests in Peru

Many global communities lack the resources to fully develop and flourish. Help to rebuild these communities by working to alleviate poverty, provide health and education resources, and promote local culture.

New Destination Opening

  • Finland Feb 2023
    Long-Term Quest to Finland has opened for 6-month to 1-year travels in February 2023. You can choose from children and education and social work volunteer project areas.
  • Italy July 2023
    Short-Term Quests to Italy have opened for 2 – 12 weeks travels. Volunteers can support foster care projects located in Northern Italy, living near or on site with the family. Volunteer Abroad Quests to Italy are available year-round.
  • Spain Dec 2023
    Short-Term Quests to Spain are now open for volunteer abroad programs. You can choose from children and education, environmental sustainability, and conservation volunteer project areas.
